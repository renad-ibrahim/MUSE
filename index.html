How it works ?

Install

muse monitor gets installed in your electrical panel. smart plugs on your appliances.

Read

The monitor and smart plugs read the electrical current being drawn and send the data to the cloud.

Analyze

muse analyzes the data and sends it back through the app on your phone or desktop.

Change

Over time, you know more about your consumption habbits and start to change them.
